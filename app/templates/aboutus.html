About Us

About Life Blood Squad

Life Blood Squad is an initiative to simplify and organize blood donations. Our platform connects donors with those in urgent need of blood, facilitating a faster, more efficient process that saves lives. We work alongside hospitals, blood banks, and community partners to bridge the gap in blood donations and make it easy for donors to help.

About Youth Red Cross (YRC)

The Youth Red Cross (YRC) is a voluntary humanitarian organization focused on youth involvement in service and community welfare. We encourage young individuals to participate in activities that promote health, aid disaster relief, and support social welfare. Our mission is to inspire responsibility and compassion through service.

How We Work

Our team verifies each blood request and coordinates with our network of donors. Requests are matched based on blood group and availability, and our technology ensures requests reach potential donors swiftly. We also provide resources and information to encourage safe and frequent blood donations within the community.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to save lives by making blood donations accessible to all, anytime and anywhere. We envision a world where no one has to wait for blood during critical situations, with the power of a connected and compassionate community ready to support each other.
